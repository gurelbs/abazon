Product description

Ultra-fast responsiveness

Accelerate everything you do with the IdeaCentre AIO 3 powered by AMD Ryzen 3 4300U Mobile Processor with Radeon Graphics. With 4 cores and industry-leading AMD technology, it makes multitasking a breeze.

Storage for large households

Your entire family’s massive media library can now fit on your hard drive. Packing up to 256 GB of disk space, that’s 4,000 hours of music or 50,000 photos. The solid-state drive also dramatically speeds up load times for apps, movies and everything in between, while allowing for lightning fast file-transfers.

Ample port selection

With more than enough ports to plug in your peripherals, the IdeaCentre AIO 3 has something for every device, with USB 3.1 and 2.0 ports that come as standard, as well as a 3-in-1 card reader and HDMI Output. That means you can set up a second screen so you can do several things at once.

A wide-angle visual experience

Get more screen real estate to watch your shows, game or do your work. The borderless design boasts a generous 90% screen-to-body ratio, a FHD display and wide-angle views, affording viewers vibrant and dynamic color from any angle.

Your privacy guaranteed

The IdeaCentre AIO 3 puts a premium on your privacy, so you can have peace of mind. Its webcam is fitted with a TrueBlock Privacy Shutter, so that you can manually close it with a single touch of the button whenever you’re not using it.
